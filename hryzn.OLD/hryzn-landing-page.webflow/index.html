FAQ

If you have any questions, we may have some answers for you. Here's some of our frequently asked questions.

What exactly is Hryzn?
Hryzn is simply a social platform designed to help connect you with your people. Rather than a network-based approach, we oriented our site around the idea of micro-communities and how sharing similar interests with others can drive us together.
Why Web 3.0?
Well, we firmly believe that Web 3.0 is going to be the new standard of the internet. We want to be a part of the ecosystem that will eradicate corporate greed and one day make the world a better place.
What makes Hryzn different?
Not only is Hryzn a cool social platform, but with a Web 3.0 approach we are providing new ways for people to protect their data, take back control, and make some money doing so.
What the $%*# is a NFT?
In simple terms, think of an NFT as a way to create something with unique value and provable scarcity, and sell ownership without the data being messed around with since it's on the blockchain. A NFT can be anything no matter if it's content, plots of virtual land, profile themes, or even a 3D virtual Hryzn group. It may sound a little confusing at first, but think of it as supply and demand: the less there is of something and the more people want it, the value of it will rise. Plus, you have the power of Crypto technology backing it up.
Now, what the $%*# is a social token?
Again in simple terms, a social token is a new concept coming out that will allow creators the opportunity of having their supporters invest in them similar to shares of stock. A creator can do something called tokenize, where they can create their own crypto token. Think of this similar to how cryptocurrencies and shares of stock work. The token of that creator can be listed at a set price of X U.S. dollars per token, and to follow a creator, a supporter simply purchases their token. Now, when the creator gets more popular and has more supporters, the value of the token rises as well, similar to when a company is doing good and their stock price goes up. Those original supporters who bought a token at a low price, now reap the benefits of that creator's value rising. So, social tokens are a new way to literally invest in your favorite creators.
